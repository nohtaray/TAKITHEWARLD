Takizawa Watermark

Watermelon produced by Takizawa boasts No.1 shipment quantity in the prefecture, known as "Takizawa Suika”. Farmland of drained volcanic ash soil and large temperature difference between day and night will nurture good quality watermelon.
A summer feast that enriches plenty of sweet juice and enjoying a refreshing texture that was juicy.
When summer arrives every year, a lot of watermelons line up and direct store people's attention to the roadside direct sales department.
It is a special product with fans throughout the country as a summer tradition in Japan.

Iwana [grew up in underwater flow of Iwate Mountain]

At the foot of Mt.Iwate volcano, aquaculture of rockfish using abundant spring water has been done since long time ago.
In the clear stream of the fish farm, rocky farms raised from eggs demonstrate its taste even with salt-grilling, sashimi and sushi as well.

~ Golden dense apple ~ Haruka Takizawa

"Haruka" was born in Iwate University's Faculty of Agriculture in 2002 and is a new varietal apple nurtured at Takizawa Farm.
Skin with faint lemon yellow. Rich flavor and crisp texture. In fact it enters plenty of honey and boasts outstanding sugar content.
Some of the sugar content of "Haruka" is 15 degrees or more on average, and some are more than 20 degrees (Fuji is about 13 degrees).
The years of hard work of Iwate's apple farmers and researchers finally ended up becoming a high perfection masterpiece.
※~ Golden dense apple ~ Haruka Takizawa is a brand item.

Quick Suite~Sweet potatoes grown in northern area~

"Quick sweet" is a sweet potato hybridization Kyushu No. 30 to Veneered.The name of the quick suite comes from the characteristic that it is a sweet variety that allows short heating cooking using a microwave oven etc. because the temperature range of saccharification is wide.A common sweet potato is a texture of Hokuhoku. However, Quick Suites are moist and moist and soft texture.
And the outstanding sweetness is as if sweet itself.
Bake, steam, boil, fry ... In all heating methods sugar content of Quick Suite is superior to other varieties.
